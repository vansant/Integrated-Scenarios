CLIMATE MODELING

Questions:
  • What will the temperature be?
  • How much will it precipitate?
  • What will the humidity be like?
  • Will there be changes in radiation?
  • How strong will the wind be?
HYDROLOGY MODELING

Outputs:
  • What will the evapotranspiration be like?
  • What will the soil moisture be like?
  • How much runoff will there be?
  • How much precipitation will fall as snow?
VEGETATION MODELING

Outputs:
  • How will the vegetation type and net production change?
  • How will fire affect the landscape in terms of area burned, fine fuel moisture?
  • How will the carbon pools change (carbon stored in live/dead wood, forest litter,soil,vegetation)?
