ULM Land Surface Model (NOAH)


ULM uses the NOAH(Ek et al., 2003) model as its land surface model(LSM), responsible for the vegetation, snow, frozen soil, and evapotranspiration components. The NOAH LSM imulates soil moisture (both liquid and frozen), soil temperature, skin temperature, snowpack depth, snowpack water equivalent (and hence snowpack density), canopy water content, and the energy flux and water flux terms of the surface energy balance and surface water. NOAH is used in most of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) coupled weather and climate models.
